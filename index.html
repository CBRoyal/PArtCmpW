Have you ever imagined that you can make friends with some unusual, strange living creatures and view them as an art. Well now you can with these wonderful and intricate masterpieces of these unusual, strange living creatures known as Pokemon.These masterpieces represents the wonderful and strange world of Pokemon and how they live among the same paths and environments as us.

Humans and Pokemon might have different paths and ways of surviving and thriving, but to us they're a part of our world. These creatures live amongst families of their own but their real family in life is US!!! . When we form bonds together with Pokemon they become a true part of our world. These artpieces signify that Pokemon do live and are a part of our world.

Why Is This Collection Special

  • Pokemon is a pretty popular video gaming franchise.
  • Pokemon live and act like us.
  • Each Pokemon represents at least an element of our world.
  • We include anybody and anything into our artpieces.
  • These masterpieces represent that Pokemon are a part of our world and we're a part of the Pokemon world.

For more information about the Pokemon world go to: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon
